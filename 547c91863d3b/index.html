The Integration Pitch

Why BridgeGate, why now

River's Edge Hospital's core system is Likely Epic (via Allina Health Community Connect) — even a single-EHR shop like this one still has to reconcile lab, imaging, pharmacy, and payer data that live outside the EHR proper. Public content is almost entirely PR/community-relations with essentially zero IT/interoperability disclosure — either a very quiet, small in-house IT footprint or fully outsourced/managed technology with no public visibility; a direct discovery call would surface more than further web research.

With a lean IT team stretched thin, the pitch isn't 'do more work' — it's 'let BridgeGate and AI carry the integration load so your team doesn't have to.'

The payoff is concrete: one integration layer retires the redundant interfaces and manual data movement that quietly drain the budget, streamlining operations and taking roughly 35% out of what a hospital your size spends on IT.
