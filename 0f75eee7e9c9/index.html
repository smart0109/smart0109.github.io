The Integration Pitch

Why BridgeGate, why now

Golisano Children's Hospital of Southwest Florida's core system is Epic (Lee Health enterprise EHR) — even a single-EHR shop like this one still has to reconcile lab, imaging, pharmacy, and payer data that live outside the EHR proper.

Even where the stack looks tidy on paper, BridgeGate's job is making sure every one of these systems agrees on the same patient record.

The payoff is concrete: one integration layer retires the redundant interfaces and manual data movement that quietly drain the budget, streamlining operations and taking roughly 35% out of what a hospital your size spends on IT.
