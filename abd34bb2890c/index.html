Why BridgeGate, why now

Rutherford Regional Health System is running Likely MEDITECH, Verisma side by side. Every one of those speaks a different dialect of HL7/FHIR/X12 — which means someone on staff is today doing by hand (or not doing at all) what BridgeGate does automatically: keeping one patient's record consistent everywhere it needs to show up. Active vendor churn: legacy 'Syntellis' patient portal replaced by InteliChart (hard cutover April 30, 2026) alongside the new Verisma ROI vendor — a live, dated patient-access/HIM systems transition.
